-The game has two player playing in the round.

-In each time a player rolls a dice as many times as he wishes.Each results get added to his round score.

-But if the player rolls a 1,all this round score get losts.After that its next players turn.

-The player choose to hold which means that his round score get added to his global score.After that its next player turn.

-The first player to reach 100 points on global score will win.
